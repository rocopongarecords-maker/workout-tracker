Features

Everything you need to train smarter

Built for real lifters who want a clean, fast, no-nonsense tool to track their progress.

🏋️

Smart Tracking

Log every set with weight and rep tracking. Auto-fills from your previous session so you spend less time typing and more time lifting.

⏱️

Rest Timer

Built-in rest timer with configurable durations and haptic alerts. Never guess your rest periods again.

📊

Detailed Analytics

Volume trends, PR tracking, muscle distribution, mood charts, and workout frequency — all in beautiful, glanceable views.

☁️

Works Offline

No internet required. Your data lives on your device first. Optional cloud sync keeps everything backed up across devices.
